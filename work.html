My Work Experience

During my academic journey, I have had the opportunity to gain valuable work experience. In the second semester of my diploma program, I ventured into the role of a seller for roadside chocolate moist cakes during the holidays. I received a daily salary of RM30, but unfortunately, my time there was cut short to just a week due to an outbreak of Covid-19 among the cake factory staff.
Undeterred, I then embarked on a position at a Siamese restaurant where my responsibilities primarily involved dishwashing. For my efforts, I earned a daily wage of RM45. Following that, during the third semester break, I joined the Pekula Jaya High School canteen team. In this role, I not only took charge of washing dishes and cups but also managed the food counter for male students. For my contributions, I was compensated with a rewarding daily wage of up to RM50.
Lastly, I had the valuable opportunity to serve as an Industrial Training student at the esteemed Sultan Badlishah Library. This experience spanned over a month and provided me with firsthand exposure to the workings of the library sector, further enriching my professional acumen.
